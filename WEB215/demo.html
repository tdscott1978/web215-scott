"Bringing the Up North experience to you"

The Idea

I'm from Philadelphia, and I've had a hard time finding a barber that I like (I finally found one from Philadelphia). Speaking with other people from up north, I found that they had a similar experience with finding a barbershop experience from home. So I decided to make a barbershop that would stand out to the many people from the northeast who have relocated to Charlotte.

The Theme

I decided to use the New York City, MTA subway as inspiration. It is a classic reminder of riding the subways back home. I also made a logo/button that was inspired my the MTA logo. I used the red,blue, and silver to incorporate the barbershop sign color theme. I also used a red/gray/blue gradient to simulate the barber pole.

Fonts

Helvetica

Used for header and menu, a callback to MTA font.

BebasNeue

I chose this for H1 because people (used to) read the paper on the train, and the bold all caps reminds me of newspaper headlines.

Lato

I went with this because I felt it was complimentary to Helvetica and did not clash like most other fonts.
